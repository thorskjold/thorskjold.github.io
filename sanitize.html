Clear streets, clear minds.
Biohazards belong in the bin
Screenshots
Capture images of hazardous materials, and raise awareness within your local community.
SanITize utilizes geolocation to pinpoint biohazards on an interactive map.
Users capture images and categorize hazards, which sync to our database.
Awareness is raised by alerting users to nearby potential hazards.

SanITize 1.0.0
  January 4, 2021
Application is fully functional, with the ability to make an account, capture hazards, and have them appear on the map.
Due to the nature of this project, no future updates should be expected, and no additional features have been planned.
